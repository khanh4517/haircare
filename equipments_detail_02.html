Corrale Hair Straightener

A cord-free straightener with flexing copper plates to give you the ultimate in control to achieve styling with less heat, reduced frizz, and fewer flyaways.

Key Benefits:

  • Cord-free, with the thermal performance of a corded straightener
  • 50 percent less breakage
  • Less frizz and fewer flyaways

Key features:

  • Suitable for all hair types: Three precise heat settings (330°F, 365°F, and 410°F) to suit your hair type, length, and desired style.
  • Manganese copper plates: Manganese copper alloy selected for its flexibility, strength, and thermal conduction.
  • Up to 30 minutes of cord-free styling***: Delivers the same thermal performance of a corded straightener. Fully recharges in just 70 minutes.
  • Engineered for balance: Weight is balanced in the hand to give maximum control while styling.
  • OLED Screen: Displays battery level, temperature control, and charging status during use.
  • Auto shut-off and Safety lock: Switches off after 10 minutes of inactivity for added peace of mind. Safety lock allows for immediate storage after use.
  • Ready to travel: Universal voltage and a battery disabler that is TSA-approved and safe to fly with.

How to Use:

Tips and tricks for using the Dyson Corrale™ straightener for the first time:

-First time use. Gently slide instructions off of the machine before inserting the flight-ready tag.

-Slide the lock button up to open your Dyson Corrale™ straightener and insert the Flight-ready tag. Should the flight-ready tag detach from the machine, hold the end of the cord with your fingers or tweezers and push the barbed end back into the hole on the machine.

Charging dock:

-Assemble the charging dock by putting the two pieces together and attaching the cable to the dock. Place Corrale on the dock to charge the machine.

-Charge to 100 percent. For best results, always start styling with 100 percent charge.

Set temperature:

-Use the + or - button to select your desired styling temperature.

-You will hear another chime when Corrale reaches your desired temperature.

-Dyson recommends you start at a lower temperature than usual.
