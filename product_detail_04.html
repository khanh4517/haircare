John Frieda Frizz-Ease Nourishing Oil Elixir

Nourish dry, damaged, and frizzy hair with this luxurious hair oil elixir. Enriched with Argan oil, plus heat protection. Expertly formulated with a special blend of silicones hydrate your hair and prevent humidity from ruining your style. For soft, lustrous hair that’s 50% smoother*. Our hair oil is suitable for use on all levels of frizz. Safe for color-treated and chemically treated hair.

Ingredients:

Cyclopentasiloxane, dimethiconol, dimethicone, bis-hydroxy/methoxy amodimethicone, fragrance, argania spinosa kernel oil.

How to Use:

Dispense one pump of our nourishing hair oil into your palm. Work through wet or dry hair to control frizz. Avoid applying directly to roots. Once applied, do not rinse out. Style as desired. To define curls and waves, start by applying our nourishing hair oil to clean hair. Then, add a styling product for curly hair like our curl reviving mousse or our hair gel for curly hair to damp hair. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to finish your style.
