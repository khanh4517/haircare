3-in-1 Ceramic Curling Iron 1.25 Inch (1 piece)

Harry Josh® Pro Tools 3-in-1 Ceramic Curling Iron 1.25” creates a variety of salon-worthy looks with just one tool. Featuring a detachable marcel clamp and detachable spring clamp, this 3-in-1 professional-grade device also provides the fluidity of a wand. All together, it accommodates every hair type and condition to achieve perfectly defined curls, beach waves or a tousled look with one unique tool. Designed with nano-ceramic and tourmaline, the barrel emits negative ions that maintain smooth hair while enhancing hair's natural body and bounce. Digital technology provides fast heat-up time and customizable heat settings accomodate all hair types and conditions for smooth styling.

Key Benefits:

  • Tool works interchangably as a wand, marcel and curling iron
  • Adjustable temperature settings from 190°F–380°F (90°C-190°C)
  • 40 seconds to full heat and 60-minute auto shut-off
  • Unique design is lightweight—only 10.1 oz.
  • Universal voltage (100-240v, 50-60Hz, 50W) for worldwide use
  • 9 ft. professional-length cord has a 360° swivel

How to Use:

Marcel clip provides extra control for use as a curling iron, but may be removed for use as curling wand for additional versatility.

To remove either spring clip or marcel clip from the barrel:

  • Turn the SPRING or MARCEL dial towards the OFF mark on the barrel.
  • Lightly pull and slide the clip off the barrel of the wand.

Attach either spring clip or marcel clip to barrel:

  • Slide the preferred clip on the barrel of the curling iron, making sure the grooves of the clip slide on the handle.
  • Turn the dial towards the SPRING or MARCEL arrow, depending on the chosen attachment.
