Hanz de Fuko Scheme Cream

Most traditional hair creams are oilier than a mechanic's handshake and heavier than Don Draper's conscience. Instead, this water-soluble, paraben-free formula taps a combination of beeswax, plant-derived oils, and nourishing extracts to make your hair feel as good as it looks…which is not like an extra from Grease.

Ingredients:

Purified Water, Mineral Wax, Helianthus Annuus Oil, Beeswax, Vitis Vinifera Seed Extract, Triticum Vulgare Starch, Pyrus Malus Fruit Extract, Phospholipids, Mentha Viridis Leaf Oil, Malva Sylvestris Extract, Hydrolyzed Soy Protein, Hydrolyzed Rice Protein, Glyceryl Stearate, Eucalyptus Oil, Equisetum Arvense Extract, Citrus Medica Limonum Peel Oil, Camellia Sinensis Extract, Ascorbyl Palmitate, Arctium Lappa Root Extract, Glycerin, Carbomer, Vitis Vinifera Seed Oil, Sesamum Indicum Seed Oil, Helianthus Annuus Seed Extract, Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract,

How to Use:

Apply to damp hair for a controlled, just-out-of-the-shower look, or to dry hair for a slightly shiny, bed-head style. All you need is a pinch.
